SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into with an effective date of January 1, 2026,
between Acme Consulting LLC ("Provider") and Globex Corporation ("Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider agrees to deliver software consulting services as described in Exhibit A.

2. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to keep confidential all proprietary information disclosed by the other
party during the term of this Agreement, and will not disclose such information to any
third party without prior written consent.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Provider a monthly fee of $10,000, due within thirty (30) days of invoice
(Net 30). Any amount not paid on time shall be subject to a late payment penalty of 1.5%
per month on the outstanding balance.

4. DATA HANDLING
Provider may process limited personal data of Client's employees solely to perform the
services described herein. Provider will not share personal information with third parties
without consent, except as required by law.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice. Client may
also terminate immediately in the event of a material breach that remains uncured for
fifteen (15) days after written notice.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to
its conflict of law provisions.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential
damages arising out of this Agreement.
